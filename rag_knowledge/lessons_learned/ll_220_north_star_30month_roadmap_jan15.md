## Section 3: Strategy Evolution by Phase

### Phase 1: Foundation Building (Months 1-6)
**Capital Range**: $5,000 - $12,000

#### Strategy
- **Ticker Focus**: SPY only (best liquidity, tightest spreads)
- **Position Size**: 1 spread at a time, no exceptions
- **Spread Type**: Bull put spreads (30-delta short / 20-delta long)
- **DTE**: 30-45 days to expiration
- **Width**: $5 wide ($500 collateral, ~$50-70 premium)

#### Objectives
1. Build trading discipline and emotional control
2. Track every trade meticulously (win rate, avg win, avg loss)
3. Achieve 70%+ win rate on paper before using real capital
4. Learn to manage losing trades (close at 2x credit)

#### Monthly Trading Cadence
- Week 1: Open spread (Monday-Tuesday)
- Week 2: Monitor, roll if needed
- Week 3: Close at 50% profit if reached
- Week 4: Let expire worthless or close

#### Risk Rules
- Max 1 spread open at any time
- Max risk: 5% of capital (~$250-600)
- Stop loss: Close at 2x credit received
- No trading 5 days before/after Fed meetings

---

### Phase 2: Scaling Up (Months 7-12)
**Capital Range**: $12,000 - $20,000

#### Strategy Changes
- **Add IWM**: Now trade both SPY and IWM
- **Position Size**: Up to 2 spreads simultaneously (on different underlyings)
- **Spread Width**: Can increase to $10 wide ($1,000 collateral, ~$100-140 premium)
- **DTE**: 30-45 days (no change)

#### Objectives
1. Validate strategy works across market conditions
2. Learn to manage multiple positions
3. Introduce sector diversification (SPY = large cap, IWM = small cap)
4. Build to $200-400/month income

#### Monthly Trading Cadence
- Week 1: Open SPY spread
- Week 2: Open IWM spread (stagger entries)
- Week 3: Manage both positions
- Week 4: Close winners, roll or close losers

#### Risk Rules
- Max 2 spreads open (1 SPY, 1 IWM)
- Total risk: max 10% of capital spread across both
- Never both positions on same underlying
- Keep 60%+ of capital as reserve

### Phase 3: Professional Scaling (Months 13-24)
**Capital Range**: $20,000 - $45,000

#### Strategy Changes
- **Add Iron Condors**: Sell both put spread AND call spread on same underlying
- **Position Size**: 3-5 spreads simultaneously
- **Strike Selection**:
  - Put spreads: 30-delta short (no change)
  - Call spreads: 16-delta short (84% OTM probability)
- **Introduce Calendar Spreads**: For low-volatility periods

#### Iron Condor Structure (Per Underlying)
```
SPY @ $500
Sell $485 Put (30-delta) / Buy $480 Put = $60 credit
Sell $520 Call (16-delta) / Buy $525 Call = $30 credit
Total credit: $90 | Total risk: $410 | Risk/reward: 4.5:1
```

#### Objectives
1. Generate $500-800/month consistently
2. Master multiple strategy types
3. Learn to adjust positions (roll, close one side)
4. Build portfolio margin eligibility (requires $100K+ eventually)

#### Monthly Trading Cadence
- Open positions every Monday (different expirations)
- Maintain 3-5 positions at various stages
- Close at 50% profit, adjust at 25% loss
- Weekly portfolio review

#### Risk Rules
- Max 5 spreads or 2 iron condors at once
- Total risk: max 15% of capital
- Correlation limit: max 2 positions on same sector
- VIX filter: Reduce size when VIX > 25

## Section 6: Failure Modes and Recovery

### If Win Rate Drops Below 60%
1. Stop trading immediately
2. Review last 10 trades for patterns
3. Return to paper trading for 30 days
4. Re-validate strategy before resuming

### If Drawdown Exceeds Monthly Limit
1. Close all positions at market
2. Take 1-week break from trading
3. Reduce position size by 50% for next month
4. Gradually rebuild after profitable month

### If Market Regime Changes (VIX > 35)
1. Close all positions within 48 hours
2. Wait for VIX to drop below 25
3. Resume with 50% normal position size
4. Return to full size after VIX stable for 2 weeks
